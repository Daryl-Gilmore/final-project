Sarge

Sarge is a 14 year old Chihuahua,he was adopted from ARF / Animal Rescue Center of Fresno Ca. He is a very loving boy. He has a great personality and loves to play. He has a big dog attitude and thanks he's invisible. He loves to rough-house and his favorite treat is carrots. Sarge went through training at Petco,Fresno Ca.
